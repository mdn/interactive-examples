overscroll-behavior-y: auto;

overscroll-behavior-y: none;

Focus your cursor on the smaller container. overscroll-behavior-y: auto; permits scroll chaining on the y-axis (vertically) after reaching the end of the scrolling area of the inner container. overscroll-behavior-y: contain; disables scroll chaining to the outer container for the y-axis only. Scroll chaining will still occur for the x-axis.
