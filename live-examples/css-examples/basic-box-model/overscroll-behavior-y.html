overscroll-behavior-y: auto;
overscroll-behavior-y: contain;
overscroll-behavior-y: none;

overscroll-behavior-y: auto; permits continuous scrolling on the y-axis after reaching the limit of the child container. overscroll-behavior-y: contain; disables continuous scrolling on the y-axis only (still permissible on the x-axis).
