London. Michaelmas term lately over and the Lord Chancellor sitting in Lincoln's Inn Hall.

Implacable November weather. As much mud in the streets as if the waters had but newly retired from the face of the earth It would not be wonderful to meet a Megalosaurus forty feet long or so

waddling like an elephantine lizard up Holborn Hill.
